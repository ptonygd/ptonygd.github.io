You earn credits by solving words — 1–3 credits per solve depending on speed. You can also watch a short video ad in the hint menu to earn 5 free credits.

🃏 About This App

Video Poker Mania ("the App", "we", "us") is a single-player, entertainment-only video poker game. There is no real-money gambling, no real cash payouts, and no account sign-up required. This policy explains what limited data the App handles, why, and your choices.

📦 Information We Collect

On your device (local-only):

The following data lives only on your device and is never sent to us:

Hands played Biggest win Win streak Achievement progress Daily streak Sound preference Haptics preference Currency display Player name Credit balance Purchase history

Stored in iOS UserDefaults and optionally synced via your private iCloud Key-Value Store. We never see this data; it never leaves Apple's ecosystem.

Advertising identifiers (only with your consent):

If you tap Allow on the App Tracking Transparency prompt, the App passes Apple's IDFA to Google AdMob so ads are more relevant. If you tap Ask App Not to Track, ads still appear but are not personalized — we do not collect the IDFA in that case.

Game Center:

If you sign in to Game Center, Apple shares your Game Center alias so leaderboards and achievements work. Score submissions go to Apple's Game Center service — not to us.

In-App Purchases:

StoreKit handles billing entirely on Apple's side. The App receives only the product ID and grants credits — it never sees your payment method, billing address, or Apple ID email.
