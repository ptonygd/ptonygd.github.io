FAQ

My progress was lost after reinstalling

Your progress is backed up via iCloud. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID and iCloud is enabled in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Wordo.

Remove Ads purchase isn't working

Tap Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used to buy it. If the issue persists, contact us above.

How do I earn more credits?

You earn credits by solving words — 1–3 credits per solve depending on speed. You can also watch a short video ad in the hint menu to earn 5 free credits.

Game Center leaderboard isn't showing

Go to Settings → Game Center and make sure you're signed in. The leaderboard requires an active Game Center account to display global rankings.

The app crashed — what should I do?

Force-quit the app, restart it, and try again. If the crash repeats, use the contact form above. Include your device model and iOS version.

FAQ

My scores / progress was lost

Progress is saved via iCloud. Check that you're signed into the same Apple ID and that iCloud is enabled for Shade Spotter in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

The colors look wrong on my screen

Shade Spotter relies on accurate color reproduction. Try disabling True Tone and Night Shift in your Display settings, and make sure your screen brightness is at a normal level. Results may vary on older devices.

Remove Ads / purchase isn't restoring

Tap Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. Ensure you're on the same Apple ID used at time of purchase. Still stuck? Use the contact form above.

How is my score calculated?

Your score is based on how close your guess is to the target shade — the closer the match, the higher the points. Bonus points are awarded for speed and streaks.

The app crashed — what should I do?

Force-quit, restart, and try again. If the crash repeats, use the contact form and include your device model, iOS version, and what you were doing when it crashed.
