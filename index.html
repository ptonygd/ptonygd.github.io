FAQ

My TV isn't being discovered

Make sure your iPhone and TV are on the same Wi-Fi network. Zap Remote uses local network discovery — it won't work over cellular or a different Wi-Fi band (try switching from 5 GHz to 2.4 GHz if your router separates them). Also ensure you've granted Local Network permission in Settings → Privacy → Local Network → Zap Remote.

Pairing keeps failing (Samsung / LG)

On Samsung, a PIN dialog should appear on your TV — accept it within a few seconds. On LG, accept the pairing prompt on-screen. If the dialog doesn't appear, go to your TV's Settings → Network and reset the Smart Hub or enable IP Control. Then delete the TV from Zap Remote and re-add it.

Roku commands aren't working

Roku uses ECP over HTTP on port 8060. Make sure your Roku is on the same Wi-Fi and that Settings → System → Advanced system settings → External control is enabled on the Roku device.

My macros stopped working after a TV update

TV firmware updates can change how commands are processed. Try deleting and recreating the macro. If a specific command is broken, contact us with your TV brand, model, and firmware version.

Zap Remote Pro purchase isn't restoring

Tap Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used at the time of purchase. Still not working? Contact us with your transaction date.

The AI assistant isn't responding

The AI assistant requires Apple Intelligence, which is available on iPhone 15 Pro and later (and iPhone 16 series) running iOS 18.1+. Make sure Apple Intelligence is enabled in Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri. The assistant processes everything on-device — no internet connection is needed for it.

The app crashed — what should I do?

Force-quit, restart, and try again. If the crash repeats, contact us with your device model, iOS version, TV brand and model, and what you were doing when it crashed.

📡 Overview

Zap Remote ("the app", "we") does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers — because we do not run servers. Everything stays on your device.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The app stores the following locally on your device (in iOS UserDefaults) and never transmits it off-device:

TV name & IP addressTV brand & MAC addressSamsung pairing tokensLG client keysCustom macrosPinned & recent appsAI conversation historyOnboarding statusPro subscription flag

Delete all of this at any time by uninstalling the app or tapping Settings → Reset all settings inside the app.

We do not operate analytics servers, track your usage, location, advertising identifier, contacts, or browsing history, or use third-party analytics, ad networks, attribution SDKs, or trackers.

📶 Network Access

The app uses your local Wi-Fi network to discover and control TVs on the same network. Commands are sent directly from your device to the TV using each manufacturer's documented control protocol:

  • Roku ECP over HTTP
  • Samsung Tizen WebSocket
  • LG webOS WebSocket

No commands are routed through our servers. We don't have any.

🎙️ Microphone & Speech

If you use the AI assistant's voice input, the app uses your device's microphone and Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer framework. Speech recognition is performed on-device whenever your device supports it; otherwise Apple may process audio on its servers under Apple's own privacy terms. Audio is never sent to us or any third party other than Apple. We do not record, store, or retain any audio.

🤖 AI Assistant

The AI assistant uses Apple's on-device FoundationModels framework (Apple Intelligence). Your prompts and the model's responses are processed entirely on your device and never sent to us or any third-party language model provider. Your conversation history is stored only in local UserDefaults on your device.

💳 Purchases & Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We never see, handle, or store your payment information. Apple handles billing, taxes, and refunds. Your subscription status is derived from Apple's Transaction.currentEntitlements on your device.

👶 Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 13.

🗑️ Data Deletion

Because no data is ever transmitted off your device, there is nothing for us to retain or delete server-side. To erase locally-stored data, uninstall the app or use Settings → Reset all settings.
